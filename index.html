FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the lottery number generator work?

A: It uses the Web Crypto API's cryptographically secure random number generator (CSPRNG). Each number is generated completely independently and unpredictably.

Q: Are the generated numbers more likely to win?

A: All number combinations have equal probability of winning. For Lotto 645, the first prize odds are about 1/8,145,060. This tool generates fair random numbers but does not guarantee winning.

Q: What lottery types are supported?

A: Korea Lotto 645 (6 numbers from 1-45), US Mega Millions (5 numbers from 1-70 + Mega Ball 1-25), and Powerball (5 numbers from 1-69 + Powerball 1-26).

FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are uploaded photos stored on the server?

A: No, absolutely not. All image analysis is processed locally in your browser through TensorFlow.js. Photos are never sent to a server and are deleted immediately after analysis.

Q: How is dog-face vs cat-face determined?

A: A CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) model trained with Teachable Machine analyzes facial features (eye shape, face contour, expression, etc.) to calculate the probability of dog-like or cat-like features.

Q: How can I get accurate results?

A: Use a bright, clear photo looking straight at the camera. Photos with visible face and even lighting provide the best results.
